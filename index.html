About Us

The Heart of Gold Foundation is A humanitarian, charitable, philanthropic, empowerment, and social enterprise organization dedicated to transforming lives through compassion, empowerment, sustainable development, and community support.

Founded on the belief that every human being deserves dignity, opportunity, hope, and a chance to thrive regardless of their circumstances.

Through partnerships, volunteerism, and community-driven projects, Heart of Gold Foundation works to make a lasting positive impact in society.

Vision & Mission

Founder Image

Vision

To create a world where compassion transforms lives, communities thrive, and every person has access to hope, opportunity, dignity, and a better future.

Mission

To identify and support vulnerable individuals and communities through humanitarian assistance, empowerment programs, skills development, education, entrepreneurship, and sustainable social enterprises.

Our foundation works tirelessly on the ground to map out critical areas where help is urgently required. Through structured assessments and community engagement, we build systems that don't just offer temporary relief, but long-term community growth.

Our Stories

Helped Rebuild His Life.

Simon Mtonga

Simon Mtonga from Chongwe lost both of his legs and has struggled to earn a living. Despite this, he remains determined to succeed. With poultry farming experience and a poultry house already built, Simon is ready to start a chicken-rearing business but needs support to buy chicks, feed, and supplies. Your contribution can help him build a sustainable source of income and regain his independence.

Help Restore Her Sight and Hope

Twapia Compound

A young girl from Twapia Compound, Ndola, is living with a serious eye condition, including glaucoma and cataract. She depends on medication and special lenses to manage pain, reduce pressure in her eyes, and prevent her condition from worsening. Her family is doing all they can, but the cost of ongoing treatment is a heavy burden. They need support to provide essential medicine, lenses, and continued medical care. Your contribution can help ease her pain, protect her remaining vision, and give her hope for a brighter future. Join the Heart of God Foundation in supporting this child and helping restore comfort, dignity, and hope.

Help Save Baby

Ephraim Chanda

At just 1 year old, Ephraim Chanda is battling Biliary Atresia and Liver Cirrhosis. He lives in constant pain, suffering from severe swelling, jaundice, and complications that threaten his life every day. There is hope. A liver donor has been found, and arrangements are underway for a life-saving liver transplant in India. While the medical costs, flights, and accommodation have been covered, the family still needs approximately K35,000 for visa fees and other urgent travel requirements. So far, K16,570 has been raised through generous donations, but more support is urgently needed. Every contribution, no matter the amount, brings Ephraim one step closer to the treatment that could save his life. Join the Heart of God Foundation in giving Ephraim a chance to live, grow, and enjoy the childhood every child deserves..

Help a Mother Save Her Son

From Lusaka

A devoted mother is fighting every day to help her special-needs son receive the medical care he urgently needs. Despite undergoing an operation in 2024, answers remain elusive, and his condition continues to worsen. With limited resources, she works tirelessly through small salaula trading, hoping to raise enough money for specialist consultations, diagnostic tests, transportation, and treatment at a larger hospital. But the burden has become too great to carry alone. Your support can help this young boy access the medical care he deserves and give his mother something she desperately needs—hope. Join the Heart of God Foundation in helping this family find answers, healing, and a brighter future..

Help a Mother of 3 Get a Hip Replacement

A 38-year-old mother

A 38-year-old mother has battled arthritis for 10 years, and it has now damaged her hip severely. Doctors at Levy Mwanawasa Hospital have advised hip replacement surgery, but she needs implants worth about K40,000. She is in constant pain and can no longer work as a maid. As a mother of three, she is pleading for support to raise the money for surgery so she can walk again and care for her children. Your help can change her life. Please stand with her today.

Empowering Women, Protecting Children

In many communities

n many communities, women and children face daily challenges that often go unseen. Mothers struggle to provide food, education, healthcare, and a safe environment for their families, while vulnerable children are left at risk of neglect, poverty, and limited opportunities. At the Heart of God Foundation, we believe that when a woman is empowered, an entire family is strengthened. Through our Women & Child Welfare Program, we support struggling mothers with skills training, small business empowerment, educational assistance, healthcare support, and essential household needs. We also work to ensure that children have access to protection, education, nutrition, and the care they need to grow into healthy and productive adults.
